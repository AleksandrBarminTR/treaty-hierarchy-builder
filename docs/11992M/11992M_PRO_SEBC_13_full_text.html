Treaty on European Union - Protocol on the statute of the european system of central banks and of the European Central Bank - Chapter III: Organisation of the ESCB - Article 13: The President

Official Journal C 191 , 29/07/1992 P. 0071


Article 13

The President

13.1. The President or, in his absence, the Vice-President shall chair the Governing Council and the Executive Board of the ECB.

13.2. Without prejudice to Article 39, the President or his nominee shall represent the ECB externally.
