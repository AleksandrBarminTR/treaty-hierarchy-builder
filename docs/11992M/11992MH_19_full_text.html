Treaty on European Union - Title III: Provisions amending the Treaty establishing the European Coal and Steel Community - Article H.19

Official Journal C 191 , 29/07/1992 P. 0050


19) The following Article shall be inserted:

'Article 78i

Member States shall take the same measures to counter fraud affecting the financial interests of the Community as they take to counter fraud affecting their own financial interests.

Without prejudice to other provisions of this Treaty, Member States shall co-ordinate their action aimed at protecting the financial interests of the Community against fraud. To this end they shall organize, with the help of the Commission, close and regular co-operation between the competent departments of their administrations.'
