Treaty on European Union - Protocol on the statute of the european system of central banks and of the European Central Bank - Chapter V: Prudential supervision - Article 25: Prudential supervision

Official Journal C 191 , 29/07/1992 P. 0073


Article 25

Prudential supervision

25.1. The ECB may offer advice to and be consulted by the Council, the Commission and the competent authorities of the Member States on the scope and implementation of Community legislation relating to the prudential supervision of credit institutions and to the stability of the financial system.

25.2. In accordance with any decision of the Council under Article 105(6) of this Treaty, the ECB may perform specific tasks concerning policies relating to the prudential supervision of credit institutions and other financial institutions with the exception of insurance undertakings.
