Treaty on European Union - Protocol on the statute of the European Monetary Institute - Article 20: Professional secrecy

Official Journal C 191 , 29/07/1992 P. 0083


Article 20

Professional secrecy

20.1. Members of the Council of the EMI and the staff of the EMI shall be required, even after their duties have ceased, not to disclose information of the kind covered by the obligation of professional secrecy.

20.2. Persons having access to data covered by Community legislation imposing an obligation of secrecy shall be subject to such legislation.
