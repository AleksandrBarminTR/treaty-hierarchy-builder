Treaty on European Union - Protocol on the statute of the European Monetary Institute - Article 18: Staff

Official Journal C 191 , 29/07/1992 P. 0083


Article 18

Staff

18.1. The Council of the EMI shall lay down the conditions of employment of the staff of the EMI.

18.2. The Court of Justice shall have jurisdiction in any dispute between the EMI and its servants within the limits and under the conditions laid down in the conditions of employment.
