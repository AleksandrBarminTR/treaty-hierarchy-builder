Treaty on European Union - Protocol on the Economic and Social Committee and Committee of the Regions

Official Journal C 191 , 29/07/1992 P. 0094


PROTOCOL on the Economic and Social Committee and the Committee of the Regions

THE HIGH CONTRACTING PARTIES,

HAVE AGREED upon the following provision, which shall be annexed to this Treaty establishing the European Community:

The Economic and Social Committee and the Committee of the Regions shall have a common organizational structure.
