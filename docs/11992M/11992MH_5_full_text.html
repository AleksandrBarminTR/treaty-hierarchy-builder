Treaty on European Union - Title III: Provisions amending the Treaty establishing the European Coal and Steel Community - Article H.5

Official Journal C 191 , 29/07/1992 P. 0045


5) The following subparagraph shall be added to Article 18:

'The Council shall, acting by a qualified majority, determine any payment to be made instead of remuneration.'
