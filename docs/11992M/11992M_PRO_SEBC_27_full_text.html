Treaty on European Union - Protocol on the statute of the european system of central banks and of the European Central Bank - Chapter VI: Financial provisions of the ESCB - Article 27: Auditing

Official Journal C 191 , 29/07/1992 P. 0074


Article 27

Auditing

27.1. The accounts of the ECB and national central banks shall be audited by independent external auditors recommended by the Governing Council and approved by the Council. The auditors shall have full power to examine all books and accounts of the ECB and national central banks and obtain full information about their transactions.

27.2. The provisions of Article 188c of this Treaty shall only apply to an examination of the operational efficiency of the management of the ECB.
