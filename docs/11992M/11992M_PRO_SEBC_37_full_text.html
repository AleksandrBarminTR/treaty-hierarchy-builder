Treaty on European Union - Protocol on the statute of the european system of central banks and of the European Central Bank - Chapter VII: General provisions - Article 37: Seat

Official Journal C 191 , 29/07/1992 P. 0076


Article 37

Seat

Before the end of 1992, the decision as to where the seat of the ECB will be established shall be taken by common accord of the governments of the Member States at the level of Heads of State or of Government.
