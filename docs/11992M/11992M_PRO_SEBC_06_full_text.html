Treaty on European Union - Protocol on the statute of the european system of central banks and of the European Central Bank - Chapter II: Objectifs and tasks of the ESCB - Article 6: International cooperation

Official Journal C 191 , 29/07/1992 P. 0070


Article 6

International cooperation

6.1. In the field of international cooperation involving the tasks entrusted to the ESCB, the ECB shall decide how the ESCB shall be represented.

6.2. The ECB and, subject to its approval, the national central banks may participate in international monetary institutions.

6.3. Articles 6.1 and 6.2 shall be without prejudice to Article 109(4) of this Treaty.
