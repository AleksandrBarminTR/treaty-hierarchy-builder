TREATY ESTABLISHING A SINGLE COUNCIL AND A SINGLE COMMISSION OF THE EUROPEAN COMMUNITIES, CHAPTER III: FINANCIAL PROVISIONS, ARTICLE 22

Official Journal L 152 , 13/07/1967 P. 0009


++++

ARTICLE 22

AN AUDIT BOARD OF THE EUROPEAN COMMUNITIES IS HEREBY ESTABLISHED . THIS BOARD SHALL TAKE THE PLACE OF THE AUDIT BOARDS OF THE EUROPEAN COAL AND STEEL COMMUNITY , THE EUROPEAN ECONOMIC COMMUNITY AND THE EUROPEAN ATOMIC ENERGY COMMUNITY . IT SHALL EXERCISE , UNDER THE CONDITIONS LAID DOWN IN THE TREATIES ESTABLISHING THE THREE COMMUNITIES , THE POWERS AND JURISDICTION CONFERRED ON THOSE BODIES BY THESE TREATIES .
