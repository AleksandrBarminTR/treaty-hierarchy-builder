TREATY ESTABLISHING A SINGLE COUNCIL AND A SINGLE COMMISSION OF THE EUROPEAN COMMUNITIES, CHAPTER II: THE COMMISSION OF THE EUROPEAN COMMUNITIES, ARTICLE 12

Official Journal L 152 , 13/07/1967 P. 0006


++++

ARTICLE 12

APART FROM NORMAL REPLACEMENT , OR DEATH , THE DUTIES OF A MEMBER OF THE COMMISSION SHALL END WHEN HE RESIGNS OR IS COMPULSORILY RETIRED .

THE VACANCY THUS CAUSED SHALL BE FILLED FOR THE REMAINDER OF THE MEMBER'S TERM OF OFFICE . THE COUNCIL MAY , ACTING UNANIMOUSLY , DECIDE THAT SUCH A VACANCY NEED NOT BE FILLED .

SAVE IN THE CASE OF COMPULSORY RETIREMENT UNDER THE PROVISIONS OF ARTICLE 13 , MEMBERS OF THE COMMISSION SHALL REMAIN IN OFFICE UNTIL THEY HAVE BEEN REPLACED .
