TREATY ESTABLISHING A SINGLE COUNCIL AND A SINGLE COMMISSION OF THE EUROPEAN COMMUNITIES, ANNEX I: TASK ASSIGNED TO THE COMMISSION OF THE EUROPEAN COMMUNITIES

Official Journal 152 , 13/07/1967 P. 0018


++++

THE COMMISSION OF THE EUROPEAN COMMUNITIES SHALL , WITHIN THE FRAMEWORK OF ITS RESPONSIBILITIES , HAVE THE TASK OF TAKING THE NECESSARY STEPS TO RATIONALISE ITS DEPARTMENTS WITHIN A REASONABLE AND RELATIVELY SHORT PERIOD OF TIME NOT EXCEEDING ONE YEAR . TO THIS END , THE COMMISSION MAY SEEK ALL APPROPRIATE OPINIONS . TO ENABLE THE COUNCIL TO FOLLOW THE PROGRESS OF THIS OPERATION , THE COMMISSION IS REQUESTED TO REPORT PERIODICALLY TO THE COUNCIL .
