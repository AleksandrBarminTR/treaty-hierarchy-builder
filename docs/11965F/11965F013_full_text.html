TREATY ESTABLISHING A SINGLE COUNCIL AND A SINGLE COMMISSION OF THE EUROPEAN COMMUNITIES, CHAPTER II: THE COMMISSION OF THE EUROPEAN COMMUNITIES, ARTICLE 13

Official Journal L 152 , 13/07/1967 P. 0006


++++

ARTICLE 13

IF ANY MEMBER OF THE COMMISSION NO LONGER FULFILS THE CONDITIONS REQUIRED FOR THE PERFORMANCE OF HIS DUTIES OR IF HE HAS BEEN GUILTY OF SERIOUS MISCONDUCT , THE COURT OF JUSTICE MAY , OR APPLICATION BY THE COUNCIL OR THE COMMISSION , COMPULSORILY RETIRE HIM .
