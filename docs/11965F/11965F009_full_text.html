TREATY ESTABLISHING A SINGLE COUNCIL AND A SINGLE COMMISSION OF THE EUROPEAN COMMUNITIES, CHAPTER II: THE COMMISSION OF THE EUROPEAN COMMUNITIES, ARTICLE 9

Official Journal L 152 , 13/07/1967 P. 0005


++++

ARTICLE 9

A COMMISSION OF THE EUROPEAN COMMUNITIES ( HEREINAFTER CALLED THE " COMMISSION " ) IS HEREBY ESTABLISHED . THIS COMMISSION SHALL TAKE THE PLACE OF THE HIGH AUTHORITY OF THE EUROPEAN COAL AND STEEL COMMUNITY , THE COMMISSION OF THE EUROPEAN ECONOMIC COMMUNITY AND THE COMMISSION OF THE EUROPEAN ATOMIC ENERGY COMMUNITY .

IT SHALL EXERCISE THE POWERS AND JURISDICTION CONFERRED ON THOSE INSTITUTIONS IN ACCORDANCE WITH THE PROVISIONS OF THE TREATIES ESTABLISHING THE EUROPEAN COAL AND STEEL COMMUNITY , THE EUROPEAN ECONOMIC COMMUNITY AND THE EUROPEAN ATOMIC ENERGY COMMUNITY , AND OF THIS TREATY .
