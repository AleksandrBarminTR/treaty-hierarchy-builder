ARTICLE 19

THE INSTITUTIONS OF THE COMMUNITIES SHALL , FOR THE PURPOSE OF APPLYING THIS PROTOCOL , COOPERATE WITH THE RESPONSIBLE AUTHORITIES OF THE MEMBER STATES CONCERNED .
