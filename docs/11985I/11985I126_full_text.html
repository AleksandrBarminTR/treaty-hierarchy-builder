Article 126

1 . Until the end of 1995 , table wines coming from areas under vines on 1 January 1985 in the regions of Asturias , Cantabria , Galicia , Guipuzcoa and Vizcaya , and for which the list is to be determined under the conditions laid down in Article 91 , may have an actual alcoholic strength of not less than 7 % vol .

For wines whose actual alcoholic strength is less than 9 % vol , an indication of that strength must appear on the labelling .

2 . Table wines referred to in paragraph 1 may circulate only in Spanish territory .
