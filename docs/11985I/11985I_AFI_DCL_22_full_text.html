DOCUMENTS CONCERNING THE ACCESSION OF THE KINGDOM OF SPAIN AND THE PORTUGUESE REPUBLIC TO THE EUROPEAN COMMUNITIES, FINAL ACT, JOINT DECLARATION ON CERTAIN TRANSITIONAL MEASURES AND CERTAIN DATA OF AN AGRICULTURAL NATURE WITH REGARD TO SPAIN

Joint declaration

on certain transitional measures and certain data of an agricultural nature with regard to Spain

1 . The transitional measures referred to in Article 91 shall be adopted , where the need arises , in accordance with the procedures and guidelines agreed upon within the Conference .

2 . The provisions to be adopted concerning the representative or reference periods referred to in :

- Article 68 and in the Articles which refer to that Article ,

- Articles 93 ( 1 ) , 98 , 118 ( 1 ) ( second indent ) , 119 ( 1 ) , 120 ( 1 ) , 121 ( 1 ) and 122 ( 1 ) ( third indent ) ,

shall be adopted in accordance with the decisions agreed upon within the Conference .
