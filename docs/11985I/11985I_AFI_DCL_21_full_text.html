DOCUMENTS CONCERNING THE ACCESSION OF THE KINGDOM OF SPAIN AND THE PORTUGUESE REPUBLIC TO THE EUROPEAN COMMUNITIES, FINAL ACT, JOINT DECLARATION ON SPANISH QUALITY WINES PRODUCED IN SPECIFIED REGIONS

Official Journal L 302 , 15/11/1985 P. 0486


++++

Joint declaration

on Spanish quality wines produced in specified regions

Spanish wines which are considered to be quality wines produced in specified regions ( psr ) within the meaning of Community rules shall be those produced and actually protected and marketed under the designation " denominacion de origen " .
