DOCUMENTS CONCERNING THE ACCESSION OF THE KINGDOM OF SPAIN AND THE PORTUGUESE REPUBLIC TO THE EUROPEAN COMMUNITIES, FINAL ACT, DECLARATION BY THE EUROPEAN ECONOMIC COMMUNITY ON COMMUNITY AID FOR MONITORING AND SUPERVISING THE WATERS

Official Journal L 302 , 15/11/1985 P. 0493

Declaration by the European Economic Community

on Community aid for monitoring and supervising the waters

The Community confirms Community support for monitoring and supervising waters falling under the sovereignty or within the jurisdiction of Portugal , may be envisaged .
