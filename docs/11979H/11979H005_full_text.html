DOCUMENTS CONCERNING THE ACCESSION OF THE HELLENIC REPUBLIC TO THE EUROPEAN COMMUNITIES, ACT CONCERNING THE CONDITIONS OF ACCESSION OF THE HELLENIC REPUBLIC AND THE ADJUSTMENTS TO THE TREATIES, PART ONE PRINCIPLES, ARTICLE 5

ARTICLE 5

ARTICLE 234 OF THE EEC TREATY AND ARTICLES 105 AND 106 OF THE EURATOM TREATY SHALL APPLY , FOR THE HELLENIC REPUBLIC TO AGREEMENTS OR CONVENTIONS CONCLUDED BEFORE ITS ACCESSION .
