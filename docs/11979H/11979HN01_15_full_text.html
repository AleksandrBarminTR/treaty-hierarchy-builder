STATUTES OF THE EURATOM SUPPLY AGENCY ( THE COUNCIL DECISION OF 6 NOVEMBER 1958 ) ( OJ NO 27 , 6 . 12 . 1958 , P . 534/58 ) ,

AS AMENDED BY DECISION 73/45/EURATOM OF 8 MARCH 1973 ( OJ NO L 83 , 30 . 3 . 1973 , P . 20 ) .

ARTICLE V ( 1 ) AND ( 2 ) OF THE STATUTES OF THE EURATOM SUPPLY AGENCY IS REPLACED BY THE FOLLOWING :

" 1 . THE CAPITAL OF THE AGENCY SHALL BE 3 392 000 EMA UNITS OF ACCOUNT .

2 . THE CAPITAL SHALL BE DIVIDED ACCORDING TO THE FOLLOWING SCALES :

- BELGIUM : 5.66 % ,

- DENMARK : 2.83 % ,

- GERMANY : 19.81 % ,

- GREECE : 5.66 % ,

- FRANCE : 19.81 % ,

- IRELAND : 0.94 % ,

- ITALY : 19.81 % ,

- NETHERLANDS : 5.66 % ,

- UNITED KINGDOM : 19.81 % .

ARTICLE X ( 1 ) AND ( 2 ) OF THE STATUTES OF THE AGENCY IS REPLACED BY THE FOLLOWING :

" 1 . AN ADVISORY COMMITTEE FOR THE AGENCY SHALL BE SET UP COMPRISING 36 MEMBERS .

2 . SEATS SHALL BE ALLOTTED TO NATIONALS OF MEMBER STATES AS FOLLOWS :

- BELGIUM : THREE MEMBERS ,

- DENMARK : TWO MEMBERS ,

- GERMANY : SIX MEMBERS ,

- GREECE : THREE MEMBERS ,

- FRANCE : SIX MEMBERS ,

- IRELAND : ONE MEMBER ,

- ITALY : SIX MEMBERS ,

- NETHERLANDS : THREE MEMBERS ,

- UNITED KINGDOM : SIX MEMBERS . "
