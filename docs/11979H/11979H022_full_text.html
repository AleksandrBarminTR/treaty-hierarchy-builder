DOCUMENTS CONCERNING THE ACCESSION OF THE HELLENIC REPUBLIC TO THE EUROPEAN COMMUNITIES, ACT CONCERNING THE CONDITIONS OF ACCESSION OF THE HELLENIC REPUBLIC AND THE ADJUSTMENTS TO THE TREATIES, PART THREE ADAPTATIONS TO ACTS ADOPTED BY THE INSTITUTIONS, ARTICLE 22

Official Journal L 291 , 19/11/1979 P. 0021


+++++

ARTICLE 22

THE ADAPTATIONS TO THE ACTS LISTED IN ANNEX II TO THIS ACT MADE NECESSARY BY ACCESSION SHALL BE DRAWN UP IN CONFORMITY WITH THE GUIDELINES SET OUT IN THAT ANNEX AND IN ACCORDANCE WITH THE PROCEDURE AND UNDER THE CONDITIONS LAID DOWN IN ARTICLE 146 .
