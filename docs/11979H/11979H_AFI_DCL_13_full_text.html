DOCUMENTS CONCERNING THE ACCESSION OF THE HELLENIC REPUBLIC TO THE EUROPEAN COMMUNITIES, FINAL ACT, DECLARATION BY THE HELLENIC REPUBLIC ON MONETARY QUESTIONS

Official Journal L 291 , 19/11/1979 P. 0190


+++++

IN ORDER THAT THE MOVEMENT OF THE REAL RATE OF THE GREEK DRACHMA , PARTICULARLY IN RELATION TO THE CURRENCIES OF THE PRESENT MEMBER STATES , MAY BE FOLLOWED ON FOREIGN EXCHANGE MARKETS , THE HELLENIC REPUBLIC WILL , BEFORE ACCESSION TO THE COMMUNITY :

- SET UP A FOREIGN EXCHANGE MARKET IN ATHENS ,

- TAKE THE NECESSARY MEASURES IN ORDER TO ENSURE THAT IN AT LEAST ONE OF THE FOREIGN EXCHANGE MARKETS OF THE COMMUNITY AS AT PRESENT CONSTITUTED , THE DRACHMA IS THE SUBJECT OF AN OFFICIAL QUOTATION , WHERE SUCH QUOTATION EXISTS , OR OF A QUOTATION OF SIMILAR TYPE .
