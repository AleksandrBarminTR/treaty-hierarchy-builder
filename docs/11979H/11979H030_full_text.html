ARTICLE 30

CUSTOMS DUTIES ON EXPORTS AND CHARGES HAVING EQUIVALENT EFFECT SHALL BE ABOLISHED BETWEEN THE COMMUNITY AS AT PRESENT CONSTITUTED AND GREECE ON 1 JANUARY 1981 .
