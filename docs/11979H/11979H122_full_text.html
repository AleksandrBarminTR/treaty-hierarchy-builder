ARTICLE 122

THE PROVISIONS OF ARTICLES 118 AND 119 SHALL APPLY TO ANY NEW AGREEMENT THAT THE COMMUNITY CONCLUDES WITH THE AFRICAN , CARIBBEAN AND PACIFIC COUNTRIES BEFORE THE ENTRY INTO FORCE OF THIS ACT .
