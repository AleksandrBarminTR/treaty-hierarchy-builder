Official Journal L 291 , 19/11/1979 P. 0128

1 . COUNCIL REGULATION ( EEC ) NO 2895/77 OF 20 DECEMBER 1977 ( OJ NO L 337 , 27 . 12 . 1977 , P . 7 ) .

IF NECESSARY ARTICLE 1 WILL HAVE TO BE AMENDED TO ADD THOSE REGIONS OF GREECE WHICH WILL QUALIFY FOR A HIGHER RATE OF INTERVENTION FROM THE EUROPEAN SOCIAL FUND .

2 . COUNCIL REGULATION ( EEC ) NO 1408/71 OF 14 JUNE 1971 ( OJ NO L 149 , 5 . 7 . 1971 , P . 2 ) .

ANNEX II OF THE REGULATION MUST BE AMENDED IN SO FAR AS THE CONCLUSION OF AN AGREEMENT BETWEEN THE COMPETENT AUTHORITIES OF THE MEMBER STATES AND THE HELLENIC REPUBLIC ON THE MAINTENANCE OF CERTAIN PROVISIONS OF BILATERAL AGREEMENTS SO REQUIRE .

3 . COUNCIL REGULATION ( EEC ) NO 574/72 OF 21 MARCH 1972 ( OJ NO L 74 , 27 . 3 . 1972 , P . 2 ) .

THE ANNEXES OF THE REGULATION MUST BE AMENDED IN SO FAR AS THE CONCLUSION OF AN AGREEMENT BETWEEN THE COMPETENT AUTHORITIES OF THE MEMBER STATES AND THE HELLENIC REPUBLIC ON THE MAINTENANCE OF CERTAIN PROVISIONS OF BILATERAL AGREEMENTS SO REQUIRE .
