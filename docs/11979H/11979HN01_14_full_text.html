" . . . IN THE ANNEX ; IN THE CASE OF GREECE , DATA RELATING TO ALL THE VARIABLES LISTED IN THE ANNEX SHALL BE COLLECTED , BEGINNING WITH THE SURVEY CARRIED OUT DURING THE YEAR FOLLOWING THAT OF ITS ACCESSION AND RELATING TO THE YEAR OF ITS ACCESSION " .
