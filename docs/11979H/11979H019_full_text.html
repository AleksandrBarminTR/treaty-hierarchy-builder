ARTICLE 19

THE FOLLOWING SHALL BE SUBSTITUTED FOR THE FIRST SUB-PARAGRAPH OF ARTICLE 134 ( 2 ) OF THE EURATOM TREATY :

" THE COMMITTEE SHALL CONSIST OF 28 MEMBERS , APPOINTED BY THE COUNCIL AFTER CONSULTATION WITH THE COMMISSION . "
