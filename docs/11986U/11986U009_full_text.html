SINGLE EUROPEAN ACT, TITLE II: PROVISIONS AMENDING THE TREATIES ESTABLISHING THE EUROPEAN COMMUNITIES, CHAPTER II: EEC, SECTION I: INSTITUTIONAL PROVISIONS, ARTICLE 9

Official Journal L 169 , 29/06/1987 P. 0006


++++

ARTICLE 9

THE SECOND PARAGRAPH OF ARTICLE 238 OF THE EEC TREATY SHALL BE REPLACED BY THE FOLLOWING PROVISION :

" THESE AGREEMENTS SHALL BE CONCLUDED BY THE COUNCIL , ACTING UNANIMOUSLY AND AFTER RECEIVING THE ASSENT OF THE EUROPEAN PARLIAMENT WHICH SHALL ACT BY AN ABSOLUTE MAJORITY OF ITS COMPONENT MEMBERS . " .
