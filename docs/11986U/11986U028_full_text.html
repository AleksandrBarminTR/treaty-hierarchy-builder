SINGLE EUROPEAN ACT, TITLE II: PROVISIONS AMENDING THE TREATIES ESTABLISHING THE EUROPEAN COMMUNITIES, CHAPTER IV: GENERAL PROVISIONS, ARTICLE 28

Official Journal L 169 , 29/06/1987 P. 0012


++++

ARTICLE 28

THE PROVISIONS OF THIS ACT SHALL BE WITHOUT PREJUDICE TO THE PROVISIONS OF THE INSTRUMENTS OF ACCESSION OF THE KINGDOM OF SPAIN AND THE PORTUGUESE REPUBLIC TO THE EUROPEAN COMMUNITIES .
