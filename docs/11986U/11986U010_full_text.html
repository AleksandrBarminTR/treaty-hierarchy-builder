SINGLE EUROPEAN ACT, TITLE II: PROVISIONS AMENDING THE TREATIES ESTABLISHING THE EUROPEAN COMMUNITIES, CHAPTER II: EEC, SECTION I: INSTITUTIONAL PROVISIONS, ARTICLE 10

Official Journal L 169 , 29/06/1987 P. 0006


++++

ARTICLE 10

ARTICLE 145 OF THE EEC TREATY SHALL BE SUPPLEMENTED BY THE FOLLOWING PROVISION :

" _ CONFER ON THE COMMISSION , IN THE ACTS WHICH THE COUNCIL ADOPTS , POWERS FOR THE IMPLEMENTATION OF THE RULES WHICH THE COUNCIL LAYS DOWN . THE COUNCIL MAY IMPOSE CERTAIN REQUIREMENTS IN RESPECT OF THE EXERCISE OF THESE POWERS . THE COUNCIL MAY ALSO RESERVE THE RIGHT , IN SPECIFIC CASES , TO EXERCISE DIRECTLY IMPLEMENTING POWERS ITSELF . THE PROCEDURES REFERRED TO ABOVE MUST BE CONSONANT WITH PRINCIPLES AND RULES TO BE LAID DOWN IN ADVANCE BY THE COUNCIL , ACTING UNANIMOUSLY ON A PROPOSAL FROM THE COMMISSION AND AFTER OBTAINING THE OPINION OF THE EUROPEAN PARLIAMENT . " .
