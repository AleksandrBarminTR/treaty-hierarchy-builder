SINGLE EUROPEAN ACT, FINAL ACT, DECLARATION BY THE COMMISSION ON ARTICLE 28 OF THE EEC TREATY

Official Journal L 169 , 29/06/1987 P. 0026

DECLARATION BY THE COMMISSION ON ARTICLE 28 OF THE EEC TREATY

WITH REGARD TO ITS OWN INTERNAL PROCEDURES , THE COMMISSION WILL ENSURE THAT THE CHANGES RESULTING FROM THE AMENDMENT OF ARTICLE 28 OF THE EEC TREATY WILL NOT LEAD TO DELAYS IN RESPONDING TO URGENT REQUESTS FOR THE ALTERATION OR SUSPENSION OF COMMON CUSTOMS TARIFF DUTIES .
