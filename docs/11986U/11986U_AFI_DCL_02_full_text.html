DECLARATION ON THE COURT OF JUSTICE

THE CONFERENCE AGREES THAT THE PROVISIONS OF ARTICLE 32D ( 1 ) OF THE ECSC TREATY , ARTICLE 168A ( 1 ) OF THE EEC TREATY AND ARTICLE 140A ( 1 ) OF THE EAEC TREATY DO NOT PREJUDGE ANY CONFERRAL OF JUDICIAL COMPETENCE LIKELY TO BE PROVIDED FOR IN THE CONTEXT OF AGREEMENTS CONCLUDED BETWEEN THE MEMBER STATES .
