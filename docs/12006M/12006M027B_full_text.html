Treaty on European Union (consolidated version) - Title V - Provisions on a common foreign and security policy - Article 27 B

Official Journal C 321 E , 29/12/2006 P. 0022 - 0022
Official Journal C 325 , 24/12/2002 P. 0020 - Consolidated version


Article 27b

Enhanced cooperation pursuant to this title shall relate to implementation of a joint action or a common position. It shall not relate to matters having military or defence implications.
