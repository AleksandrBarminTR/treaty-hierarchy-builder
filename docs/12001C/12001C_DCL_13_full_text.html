Treaty of Nice amending the Treaty on European Union, the Treaties establishing the European Communities and certa in related acts - Declarations Adopted By The Conference - Declaration on Article 225(2) and (3) of the Treaty establishing the European Community

Official Journal 080 , 10/03/2001 P. 0079 - 0079


13. Declaration on Article 225(2) and (3) of the Treaty establishing the European Community

The Conference considers that the essential provisions of the review procedure in Article 225(2) and (3) should be defined in the Statute of the Court of Justice. Those provisions should in particular specify:

- the role of the parties in proceedings before the Court of Justice, in order to safeguard their rights;

- the effect of the review procedure on the enforceability of the decision of the Court of First Instance;

- the effect of the Court of Justice decision on the dispute between the parties.
