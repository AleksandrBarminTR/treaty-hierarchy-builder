K. Declarations by various new Member States

48. Joint Declaration by the Kingdom of Norway and the Kingdom of Sweden regarding fisheries

In an exchange of letters between the Kingdom of Norway and the Kingdom of Sweden it has been agreed that Norway will maintain Swedish rights equal to those of the 1977 bilateral Fisheries Agreement. Quantities and species, in accordance with the practice of the bilateral Agreement, will be transferred on a yearly basis after bilateral consultations and in accordance with Article 9 of Council Regulation (EEC) No 3760/92.
