VII. AGRICULTURE

A. GENERAL PROVISIONS

I. FADN

365 R 0079: Council Regulation No 79/65/EEC of 15 June 1965 setting up a network for the collection of accountancy data on the incomes and business operation of agricultural holdings in the European Economic Community (OJ No 109, 23.6.1965, p. 1859), as last amended by:

- 390 R 3577: Council Regulation (EEC) No 3577/90 of 4 December 1990 (OJ No L 353, 17.12.1990, p. 23).

Norway, Finland and Sweden shall comply with the nature of accounting data and the types of holdings required pursuant to Regulation (EEC) No 79/65/EEC by 31 December 1997 at the latest.
