21. Joint Declaration on Articles 6 and 76 of the Act of Accession

The Republic of Austria and the Community confirm their intention to ensure, through appropriate negotiations, that, from the date of accession, hauliers from third countries, in particular from Slovenia and Switzerland, will not enjoy more favourable treatment than EU-hauliers in respect of the transit of heavy goods vehicles through Austrian territory.
