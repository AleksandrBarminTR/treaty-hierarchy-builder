ACT concerning the conditions of accession of the Kingdom of Norway, the Republic of Austria, the Republic of Finland and the Kingdom of Sweden and the adjustments to the Treaties on which the European Union is founded - PART FOUR : TRANSITIONAL MEASURES - TITLE II : TRANSITIONAL MEASURES CONCERNING NORWAY - CHAPTER 5 : Financial and budgetary provisions - Article 65

Official Journal C 241 , 29/08/1994 P. 0035


Article 65

Own resources accruing from VAT shall be calculated and checked as though investment tax were not applied. To that end, the Kingdom of Norway shall, upon accession, implement the procedures necessary to ensure that annual revenue derived from VAT and annual revenue derived from investment tax is accurately entered in the accounts.
