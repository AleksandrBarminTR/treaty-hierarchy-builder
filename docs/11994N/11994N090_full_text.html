ACT concerning the conditions of accession of the Kingdom of Norway, the Republic of Austria, the Republic of Finland and the Kingdom of Sweden and the adjustments to the Treaties on which the European Union is founded - PART FOUR : TRANSITIONAL MEASURES - TITLE IV : TRANSITIONAL MEASURES CONCERNING FINLAND - CHAPTER 3 : Fisheries - Section II : Access to waters and resources - Subsection I : Finnish vessels - Article 90

Official Journal C 241 , 29/08/1994 P. 0038


Subsection I Finnish vessels

Article 90

For the purposes of their integration into the Community system for fisheries and aquaculture set up by Regulation (EEC) No 3760/92, access to the waters falling under the sovereignty or within the jurisdiction of the Member States of the Union as at present constituted by fishing vessels flying the flag of Finland and recorded and/or registered in a Finnish port, hereinafter called 'Finnish vessels`, shall be subject to the system laid down in this Subsection.
