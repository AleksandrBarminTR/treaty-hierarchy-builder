V. CONSTRUCTION PLANT AND EQUIPMENT

1. 386 L 0295: Council Directive 86/295/EEC of 26 May 1986 on the approximation of the laws of the Member States relating to roll-over protective structures (ROPS) for certain construction plant (OJ No L 186, 8.7.1986, p. 1).

In Annex IV, the following is added to the text in brackets:

'A for Austria, N for Norway, S for Sweden, FI for Finland`.

2. 386 L 0296: Council Directive 86/296/EEC of 26 May 1986 on the approximation of the laws of the Member States relating to falling-object protective structures (FOPS) for certain construction plant (OJ No L 186, 8.7.1986, p. 10).

In Annex IV, the following is added to the text in the first indent:

'A for Austria, N for Norway, S for Sweden, FI for Finland`.
