50. Declaration by the Republic of Finland and the Kingdom of Sweden on alcohol monopolies

The Conference at Ministerial level was informed at its 5th meeting on 21 December 1993 of the exchange of letters between the Commission and Finland and the Commission and Sweden on alcohol monopolies under Chapter 6: Competition policy, recorded in documents CONF-SF 78/93 and CONF-S 82/93.
