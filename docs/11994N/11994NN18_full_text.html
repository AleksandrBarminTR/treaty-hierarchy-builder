Finland: 1 January 1996

Sweden: 1 January 1997

During the transitional period, no potatoes from these Member States shall be introduced into the territory of other Member States.

3. 393 L 0085: Council Directive 93/85/EEC of 4 October 1993 on the control of potato ring rot (OJ No L 259, 18.10.1993, p. 1).

Finland: 1 January 1996
