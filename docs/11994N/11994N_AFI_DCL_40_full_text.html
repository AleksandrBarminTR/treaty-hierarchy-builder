40. Declaration by the Kingdom of Norway on transparency

The Kingdom of Norway welcomes the development now taking place in the Union towards greater openness and transparency.

In Norway, open government, including public access to official records, is a principle of fundamental legal and political importance. The Kingdom of Norway will continue to apply this principle in accordance with its rights and obligations as a member of the Union.
