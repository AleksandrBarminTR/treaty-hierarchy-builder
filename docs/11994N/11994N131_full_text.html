ACT concerning the conditions of accession of the Kingdom of Norway, the Republic of Austria, the Republic of Finland and the Kingdom of Sweden and the adjustments to the Treaties on which the European Union is founded - PART FOUR : TRANSITIONAL MEASURES - TITLE V : TRANSITIONAL MEASURES CONCERNING SWEDEN - CHAPTER 4 : External relations including Customs Union - Article 131

Official Journal C 241 , 29/08/1994 P. 0044


Article 131

If the new trade agreements to be concluded between the Community and Estonia, Latvia and Lithuania have not entered into force by the date of accession, the Community shall take the necesary measures to allow on accession the continuation of the prevailing level of access to the Swedish market of products originating in those Baltic States.
