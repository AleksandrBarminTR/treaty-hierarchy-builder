ACT concerning the conditions of accession of the Kingdom of Norway, the Republic of Austria, the Republic of Finland and the Kingdom of Sweden and the adjustments to the Treaties on which the European Union is founded - PART FOUR : TRANSITIONAL MEASURES - TITLE III : TRANSITIONAL MEASURES CONCERNING AUSTRIA - CHAPTER 4 : External relations including Customs Union - Article 78

Official Journal C 241 , 29/08/1994 P. 0037


Article 78

With effect from 1 January 1995, the Republic of Austria shall withdraw, inter alia, from the Convention establishing the European Free-Trade Association signed on 4 January 1960.
