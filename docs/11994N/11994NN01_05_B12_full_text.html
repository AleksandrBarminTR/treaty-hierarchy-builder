Official Journal C 241 , 29/08/1994 P. 0129


XII. Remainder

368 R 0827: Council Regulation (EEC) No 827/68 of 28 June 1968 on the common organization of the market in certain products listed in Annex II to the Treaty (OJ No L 151, 30.6.1968, p. 16), as last amended by:

- 393 R 2430: Commission Regulation (EEC) No 2430/93 of 1 September 1993 (OJ No L 223, 2.9.1993, p. 9).

The following is added to Article 5:

'Nevertheless, subject to Commission authorization, aids for the production and marketing of reindeer and reindeer products (CN ex 0208 and ex 0210) may be granted by Finland, Norway and Sweden insofar as they do not entail any increase in traditional levels of production.`
