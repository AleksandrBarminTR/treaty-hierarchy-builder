35. Declaration on compliance with the undertakings in agricultural matters under instruments which are not included in the Act of Accession

The European Union declares that all instruments necessary for compliance with the outcome of the accession negotiations on agricultural matters which are not included in the Act of Accession (new Council instruments to be applied after accession and Commission instruments) will be adopted in due course in accordance with the relevant procedures laid down in the Act of Accession itself or in the 'acquis communautaire`.

Most of these instruments shall be adopted during the interim period, in accordance with the procedures laid down in the Act of Accession.

The other commitments on agricultural matters resulting from the negotiations shall be implemented in an expeditious and timely manner.
