5. Joint Declaration on secondary residences

Nothing in the 'acquis communautaire` prevents individual Member States from taking national, regional or local measures regarding secondary residences, provided that they are necessary for land-use planning and environmental protection and apply without direct or indirect discrimination between nationals of the Member States in conformity with the 'acquis`.
