H. Declarations by the Republic of Austria

41. Declaration by the Republic of Austria on Article 109g of the EC Treaty

The Republic of Austria notes that the composition of the ECU currency basket remains unchanged and that with the participation of the Republic of Austria in the third stage the value of the Schilling vis-Ã -vis the ECU shall be irrevocably fixed.

The Republic of Austria will continue to maintain the stability of the Schilling and in this way contribute to the realization of Economic and Monetary Union. The transition in stages to a single European currency is supported by the Republic of Austria, because the quality of the planned European currency is safeguarded by the preconditions in terms of stability policy contained in the EC Treaty.
