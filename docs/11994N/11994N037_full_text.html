ACT concerning the conditions of accession of the Kingdom of Norway, the Republic of Austria, the Republic of Finland and the Kingdom of Sweden and the adjustments to the Treaties on which the European Union is founded - PART FOUR : TRANSITIONAL MEASURES - TITLE II : TRANSITIONAL MEASURES CONCERNING NORWAY - CHAPTER 3 : Fisheries - Section II : Access to waters and resources - Article 37

Official Journal C 241 , 29/08/1994 P. 0028


Section II Access to waters and resources

Article 37

Unless any provision of this Chapter stipulates otherwise, the arrangements for access to waters laid down in this Section shall apply during a transitional period the end of which shall be marked by the date of implementation of the Community fishing permit system and which will not in any event be later than the date of expiry of the period laid down in Article 14 (2) of Council Regulation (EEC) No 3760/92 of 20 December 1992 establishing a Community system for fisheries and aquaculture.
