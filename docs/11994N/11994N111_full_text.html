ACT concerning the conditions of accession of the Kingdom of Norway, the Republic of Austria, the Republic of Finland and the Kingdom of Sweden and the adjustments to the Treaties on which the European Union is founded - PART FOUR : TRANSITIONAL MEASURES - TITLE IV : TRANSITIONAL MEASURES CONCERNING FINLAND - CHAPTER 5 : Financial and budgetary provisions - Article 111

Official Journal C 241 , 29/08/1994 P. 0041


Article 111

The Republic of Finland's share in the financing of the financial mechanism provided for in Article 116 of the Agreement on the European Economic Area shall be borne by the general budget of the European Communities.
