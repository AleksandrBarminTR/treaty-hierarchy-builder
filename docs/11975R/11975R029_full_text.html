TREATY AMENDING CERTAIN FINANCIAL PROVISIONS OF THE TREATIES ESTABLISHING THE EUROPEAN ECONOMIC COMMUNITIES AND OF THE TREATY ESTABLISHING A SINGLE COUNCIL OF THE EUROPEAN COMMUNITIES, CHAPTER V FINAL PROVISIONS, ARTICLE 29

Article 29

This Treaty shall be ratified by the High Contracting Parties in accordance with their respective constitutional requirements . The instruments of ratification shall be deposited with the Government of the Italian Republic .
