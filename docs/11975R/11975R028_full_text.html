TREATY AMENDING CERTAIN FINANCIAL PROVISIONS OF THE TREATIES ESTABLISHING THE EUROPEAN ECONOMIC COMMUNITIES AND OF THE TREATY ESTABLISHING A SINGLE COUNCIL OF THE EUROPEAN COMMUNITIES, CHAPTER V FINAL PROVISIONS, ARTICLE 28

Official Journal L 359 , 31/12/1977 P. 0017


++++

Article 28

1 . The members of the Court of Auditors shall be appointed upon the entry into force of this Treaty .

2 . The terms of office of the members of the Audit Board and that of the auditor shall expire on the day they submit their report on the financial year preceding that in which the members of the Court of Auditors are appointed ; their powers of audit shall be confined to operations relating to that financial year .
