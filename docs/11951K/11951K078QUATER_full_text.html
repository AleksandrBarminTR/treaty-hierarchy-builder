Treaty establishing the European Coal and Steel Community - Title IV - General provisions - Article 78c


Treaty establishing the European Coal and Steel Community - Title IV - General provisions - Article 78c*

* As amended by Article H(15) TEU.

The Commission shall implement the administrative budget, in accordance with the provisions of the regulations made pursuant to Article 78h, on its own responsibility and within the limits of the appropriations, having regard to the principles of sound financial management.

The regulations shall lay down detailed rules for each institution concerning its part in effecting its own expenditure.

Within the administrative budget, the Commission may, subject to the limits and conditions laid down in the regulations made pursuant to Article 78h, transfer appropriations from one chapter to another or from one subdivision to another.
