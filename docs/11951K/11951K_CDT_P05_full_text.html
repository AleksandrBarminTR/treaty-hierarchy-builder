Treaty establishing the European Coal and Steel Community - Convention on the Transitional Provisions - Part One - Implementation of the Treaty - Chapter 1 - Setting up of the institutions of the Community - The Court - Article 5

The Court shall take office upon the appointment of its members. Its first President shall be appointed in the same manner as the President of the Commission.

The Court shall adopt its Rules of Procedure within three months.

No matter may be brought before the Court until its Rules of Procedure have been published. The imposition of periodic penalty payments and the collection of fines shall be suspended until the date of that publication.

The time within which an action must be brought shall run only from the same date.
