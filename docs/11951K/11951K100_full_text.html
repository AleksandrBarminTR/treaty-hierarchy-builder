Treaty establishing the European Coal and Steel Community - Title IV - General provisions - Article 100

This Treaty, drawn up in a single original, shall be deposited in the archives of the Government of the French Republic, which shall transmit a certified copy thereof to each of the governments of the other signatory States.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the undersigned Plenipotentiaries have signed this Treaty and affixed thereto their seals.

Done at Paris this eighteenth day of April in the year one thousand nine hundred and fifty one.

ADENAUER

Paul VAN ZEELAND

J. MEURICE

SCHUMAN

SFORZA

Jos. BECH

STIKKER

VAN DEN BRINK
