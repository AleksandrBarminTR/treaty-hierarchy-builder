Treaty establishing the European Coal and Steel Community - Convention on the Transitional Provisions - Part One - Implementation of the Treaty - Chapter 1 - Setting up of the institutions of the Community - The Consultative Committee - Article 4


Treaty establishing the European Coal and Steel Community - Convention on the Transitional Provisions - Part One - Implementation of the Treaty - Chapter 1 - Setting up of the institutions of the Community - The Consultative Committee - Article 4

To enable the Consultative Committee to be set up as provided in Article 18 of the Treaty, governments shall forward to the Commission upon its taking office all information on the producers', workers' and consumers' organizations for coal and steel in each country, particularly on their membership, geographical coverage, statutes, powers and functions.

On the basis of this information, the Commission shall, within two months of taking office, obtain a decision of the Council designating the producers' and workers' associations which are to put forward candidates.

The Consultative Committee shall be set up within one month of this decision.
