Treaty establishing the European Coal and Steel Community - Title II - The institutions of the Community - Chapter 1 - The Commission - Article 18


Treaty establishing the European Coal and Steel Community - TITLE II - The institutions of the Community - Chapter 1 - The Commission - Article 18

A Consultative Committee shall be attached to the Commission. It shall consist of not less than 84 and not more than 108 members and shall comprise equal numbers of producers, of workers and of consumers and dealers.*

*First paragraph as amended by Article 25 AA A/FIN/SWE in the version resulting from Article 16 AD AA A/FIN/SWE.

The members of the Consultative Committee shall be appointed by the Council.

The Council shall, acting by a qualified majority, determine any payment to be made instead of remuneration.**

**Third paragraph as added by Article H(5) TEU.
