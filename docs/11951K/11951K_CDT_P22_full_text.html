Treaty establishing the European Coal and Steel Community - Convention on the Transitional Provisions - Part Two - Relations between the Community and third countries - Chapter 5 - Special provision - Article 22


Treaty establishing the European Coal and Steel Community - Convention on the Transitional Provisions - Part Two - Relations between the Community and third countries - Chapter 5 - Special provision - Article 22

Notwithstanding the expiry of the transitional period, trade in coal and steel between the Federal Republic of Germany and the Soviet Zone of Occupation shall be regulated, as far as the Federal Republic is concerned, by the Federal Government in agreement with the Commission.
