Treaty establishing the European Coal and Steel Community - Title II - The institutions of the Community - Chapter 5** - The Court of Auditors - Article 45a

** Chapter 5 (Articles 45a to 45c, formerly Articles 78e and 78f) as inserted by Article H(14) TEU.

The Court of Auditors shall carry out the audit.
