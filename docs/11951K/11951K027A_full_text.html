Official Journal C 191 , 29/07/1992 P. 0046


Treaty establishing the European Coal and Steel Community - Title II - The institutions of the Community - Chapter 3 - The Council - Article 27a*

As inserted by Article H(9) TEU.

The Council shall meet when convened by its President on his own initiative or at the request of one of its members or of the Commission.
