Treaty establishing the European Coal and Steel Community - Convention on the Transitional Provisions - Part Two - Relations between the Community and third countries - Chapter 4 - Liberalization of trade - Article 21


Treaty establishing the European Coal and Steel Community - Convention on the Transitional Provisions - Part Two - Relations between the Community and third countries - Chapter 4 - Liberalization of trade - Article 21

The Member States of the Community recognize that they constitute a special customs system such as is referred to in Article 5 of the Code of Liberalization of Trade of the Organization for European Economic Cooperation as in force on the date of the signature of the Treaty. They therefore agree to notify the Organization accordingly in due course.
