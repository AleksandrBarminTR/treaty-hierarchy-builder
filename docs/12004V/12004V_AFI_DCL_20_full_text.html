20. Declaration on Article III-243

The Conference notes that the provisions of Article III-243 shall be applied in accordance with the current practice. The terms "such measures are required in order to compensate for the economic disadvantages caused by the division of Germany to the economy of certain areas of the Federal Republic affected by that division" shall be interpreted in accordance with the existing case-law of the Court of Justice of the European Communities and of the Court of First Instance.
