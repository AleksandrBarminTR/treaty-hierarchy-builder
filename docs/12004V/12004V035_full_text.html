Treaty establishing a Constitution for Europe - PART I - TITLE V — EXERCISE OF UNION COMPETENCE - CHAPTER I — COMMON PROVISIONS - Article I-35

Official Journal 310 , 16/12/2004 P. 0027 - 0028


Article I-35

Non-legislative acts

1. The European Council shall adopt European decisions in the cases provided for in the Constitution.

2. The Council and the Commission, in particular in the cases referred to in articles I-36 and I-37, and the European Central Bank in the specific cases provided for in the constitution, shall adopt European regulations and decisions.

3. The Council shall adopt recommendations. It shall act on a proposal from the Commission in all cases where the Constitution provides that it shall adopt acts on a proposal from the Commission. It shall act unanimously in those areas in which unanimity is required for the adoption of a Union act. The Commission, and the European Central Bank in the specific cases provided for in the Constitution, shall adopt recommendations.
