Treaty establishing a Constitution for Europe - PART II — THE CHARTER OF FUNDAMENTAL RIGHTS OF THE UNION - TITLE II — FREEDOMS - Article II-67

Official Journal 310 , 16/12/2004 P. 0043 - 0043


Article II-67

Respect for private and family life

Everyone has the right to respect for his or her private and family life, home and communications.
