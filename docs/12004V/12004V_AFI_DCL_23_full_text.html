Treaty establishing a Constitution for Europe - FINAL ACT - A.Declarations concerning provisions of the Constitution - 23.Declaration on Article III-273(1), second subparagraph

Official Journal 310 , 16/12/2004 P. 0462 - 0462


23. Declaration on Article III-273(1), second subparagraph

The Conference considers that the European laws referred to in the second subparagraph of Article III-273(1) should take into account national rules and practices relating to the initiation of criminal investigations.
