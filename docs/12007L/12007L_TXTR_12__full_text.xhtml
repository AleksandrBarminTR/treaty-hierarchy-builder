Official Journal of the European Union

L 150/1

Procès–verbal of rectification to the Treaty of Lisbon amending the Treaty on European Union and the Treaty establishing the European Community, signed at Lisbon on 13 December 2007

( Official Journal of the European Union C 306 of 17 December 2007 )

This rectification has been carried out by means of a procès-verbal of rectification signed at Rome on 20 May 2016 with the Government of the Italian Republic as depository.

Page 175, Protocols, Part B (protocols to be annexed to the Treaty of Lisbon), Protocol No 1 Amending the Protocols Annexed to the Treaty on European Union, to the Treaty Establishing The European Community and/or to the Treaty Establishing The European Atomic Energy Community; Article 1, point (12) (regarding the Protocol on the Statute of the European Investment Bank), under (h), the following point is added:

‘(iii)

in paragraph 4, the words “Within the framework of the Treaty and this Statute” shall be replaced by “Within the framework of the Treaties and this Statute”.’
