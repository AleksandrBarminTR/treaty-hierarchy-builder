Charter of Fundamental Rights of the European Union - TITLE III - EQUALITY - Article 23 - Equality between women and men

Official Journal 303 , 14/12/2007 P. 0007 - 0007


Article 23

Equality between women and men

Equality between women and men must be ensured in all areas, including employment, work and pay.

The principle of equality shall not prevent the maintenance or adoption of measures providing for specific advantages in favour of the under-represented sex.
