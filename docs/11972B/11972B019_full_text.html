THE FOLLOWING SHALL BE SUBSTITUTED FOR THE SECOND AND THIRD PARAGRAPHS OF ARTICLE 32B OF THE ECSC TREATY , THE SECOND AND THIRD PARAGRAPHS OF ARTICLE 167 OF THE EEC TREATY AND THE SECOND AND THIRD PARAGRAPHS OF ARTICLE 139 OF THE EURATOM TREATY :

" EVERY THREE YEARS THERE SHALL BE A PARTIAL REPLACEMENT OF THE JUDGES . SIX AND FIVE JUDGES SHALL BE REPLACED ALTERNATELY .

EVERY THREE YEARS THERE SHALL BE A PARTIAL REPLACEMENT OF THE ADVOCATES-GENERAL . ONE AND TWO ADVOCATES-GENERAL SHALL BE REPLACED ALTERNATELY . "
