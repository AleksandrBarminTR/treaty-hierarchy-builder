THE FOLLOWING ARTICLE 33A IS INSERTED :

" ARTICLE 33A

1 . NEW MEMBER STATES SHALL MAKE AN INVENTORY OF THE STOCKS OF SUGAR IN FREE CIRCULATION IN THEIR TERRITORY ON THE DATE OF THE APPLICATION OF THIS REGULATION .

2 . A QUANTITY OF SUGAR WHICH MAY BE REGARDED AS REPRESENTING A NORMAL STOCK ON THE DATE REFERRED TO IN PARAGRAPH 1 SHALL BE FIXED FOR EACH NEW MEMBER STATE .

THIS QUANTITY SHALL BE FIXED TAKING INTO CONSIDERATION :

( A ) A NORMAL WORKING STOCK ,

( B ) THE ANTICIPATED CONSUMPTION IN THE MEMBER STATE CONCERNED UNTIL THE NEW BEET HARVEST ,

( C ) THE SUPPLY SITUATION BASED ON NATIONAL PRODUCTION AND IMPORTS OR EXPORTS OF THAT MEMBER STATE .

3 . WHEN THE QUANTITIES RECORDED IN THE FRAMEWORK OF THE INVENTORY REFERRED TO IN PARAGRAPH 1 EXCEED THE QUANTITY REFERRED TO IN THE FIRST SUBPARAGRAPH OF PARAGRAPH 2 , THE NECESSARY MEASURES SHALL BE TAKEN TO OBVIATE SUCH FINANCIAL BURDENS AS MAY RESULT FOR THE COMMUNITY FROM THE MARKETING OF A QUANTITY EQUIVALENT TO THE EXCESS QUANTITY .

4 . DETAILED RULES FOR THE APPLICATION OF THIS ARTICLE SHALL BE ADOPTED IN ACCORDANCE WITH THE PROCEDURE LAID DOWN IN ARTICLE 40 . "
