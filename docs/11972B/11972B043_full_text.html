DOCUMENTS CONCERNING THE ACCESSION TO THE EUROPEAN COMMUNITIES OF THE KINGDOM OF DENMARK, IRELAND, THE KINGDOM OF NORWAY AND THE UNITED KINGDOM OF GREAT BRITAIN AND NORTHERN IRELAND, ACT CONCERNING THE CONDITIONS OF ACCESSION AND THE ADJUSTMENTS TO THE TREATIES, PART FOUR TRANSITIONAL MEASURES, TITLE I FREE MOVEMENT OF GOODS, CHAPTER 2 ELIMINATION OF QUANTITATIVE RESTRICTIONS, ARTICLE 43

Official Journal L 073 , 27/03/1972 P. 0023


++++

NOTWITHSTANDING ARTICLE 42 , MEMBER STATES MAY , FOR A PERIOD OF TWO YEARS , RETAIN RESTRICTIONS ON EXPORTS OF WASTE AND SCRAP METAL OF IRON OR STEEL FALLING WITHIN COMMON CUSTOMS TARIFF HEADING NO 73.03 , INSOFAR AS THESE ARRANGEMENTS ARE NOT MORE RESTRICTIVE THAN THOSE APPLIED TO EXPORTS TO THIRD COUNTRIES .

FOR DENMARK AND NORWAY THE PERIOD SHALL BE THREE YEARS AND FOR IRELAND FIVE YEARS .
