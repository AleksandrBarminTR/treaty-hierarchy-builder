DOCUMENTS CONCERNING THE ACCESSION TO THE EUROPEAN COMMUNITIES OF THE KINGDOM OF DENMARK, IRELAND, THE KINGDOM OF NORWAY AND THE UNITED KINGDOM OF GREAT BRITAIN AND NORTHERN IRELAND, ACT CONCERNING THE CONDITIONS OF ACCESSION AND THE ADJUSTMENTS TO THE TREATIES, PART FOUR TRANSITIONAL MEASURES, TITLE VI FINANCIAL PROVISIONS, ARTICLE 129

Official Journal L 073 , 27/03/1972 P. 0041


++++

1 . THE FINANCIAL CONTRIBUTIONS FROM MEMBER STATES REFERRED TO IN ARTICLE 3 ( 2 ) OF THE DECISION OF 21 APRIL 1970 SHALL BE APPORTIONED AS FOLLOWS :

_ FOR THE NEW MEMBER STATES :

DENMARK 2.42 %

IRELAND 0.60 %

NORWAY 1.66 %

UNITED KINGDOM 19.00 %

_ AND FOR THE ORIGINAL MEMBER STATES , IN ACCORDANCE WITH THE SCALE LAID DOWN IN ARTICLE 3 ( 2 ) OF THE DECISION OF 21 APRIL 1970 , AFTER THE FINANCIAL CONTRIBUTIONS OF THE NEW MEMBER STATES SPECIFIED ABOVE HAVE BEEN DEDUCTED .

2 . FOR 1973 , THE BASIS FOR CALCULATING THE VARIATIONS REFERRED TO IN ARTICLE 3 ( 3 ) OF THE DECISION OF 21 APRIL 1970 SHALL BE :

_ FOR THE NEW MEMBER STATES , THE PERCENTAGES REFERRED TO IN PARAGRAPH 1 ;

_ FOR THE ORIGINAL MEMBER STATES , THEIR RELATIVE SHARE FOR THE PRECEDING YEAR , ACCOUNT BEING TAKEN OF THE PERCENTAGES FOR THE NEW MEMBER STATES SPECIFIED ABOVE .
