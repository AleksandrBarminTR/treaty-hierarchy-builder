DOCUMENTS CONCERNING THE ACCESSION TO THE EUROPEAN COMMUNITIES OF THE KINGDOM OF DENMARK, IRELAND, THE KINGDOM OF NORWAY AND THE UNITED KINGDOM OF GREAT BRITAIN AND NORTHERN IRELAND, ACT CONCERNING THE CONDITIONS OF ACCESSION AND THE ADJUSTMENTS TO THE TREATIES, PART FOUR TRANSITIONAL MEASURES, TITLE II AGRICULTURE, CHAPTER 2 PROVISIONS RELATING TO CERTAIN COMMON ORGANIZATIONS OF MARKETS, SECTION 15 SEEDS, ARTICLE 96

Official Journal L 073 , 27/03/1972 P. 0034


++++

WHEN AN AID IS GRANTED FOR SEED PRODUCTION , THE AMOUNT OF THE AID MAY BE FIXED , IN RESPECT OF THE NEW MEMBER STATES , AT A LEVEL DIFFERENT FROM THAT FIXED FOR THE COMMUNITY AS ORIGINALLY CONSTITUTED IF THE INCOME OF PRODUCERS IN A NEW MEMBER STATE WAS PREVIOUSLY APPRECIABLY DIFFERENT FROM THE INCOME OF PRODUCERS IN THE COMMUNITY AS ORIGINALLY CONSTITUTED .

IN THAT EVENT , THE AMOUNT OF AID IN RESPECT OF THE NEW MEMBER STATE MUST TAKE ACCOUNT OF THE INCOME PREVIOUSLY RECEIVED BY SEED PRODUCERS AND OF THE NEED TO AVOID ANY DISTORTION OF PRODUCTION PATTERNS , AND THE NEED TO ALIGN THAT AMOUNT GRADUALLY WITH THE COMMUNITY AMOUNT .
