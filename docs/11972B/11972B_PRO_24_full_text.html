DOCUMENTS CONCERNING THE ACCESSION TO THE EUROPEAN COMMUNITIES OF THE KINGDOM OF DENMARK, IRELAND, THE KINGDOM OF NORWAY AND THE UNITED KINGDOM OF GREAT BRITAIN AND NORTHERN IRELAND, PROTOCOL NO 24 ON THE PARTICIPATION OF THE NEW MEMBER STATES IN THE FUNDS OF THE EUROPEAN COAL AND STEEL COMMUNITY

Official Journal L 073 , 27/03/1972 P. 0178


++++

THE CONTRIBUTIONS OF THE NEW MEMBER STATES TO THE FUNDS OF THE EUROPEAN COAL AND STEEL COMMUNITY SHALL BE FIXED AS FOLLOWS :

UNITED KINGDOM 57,000,000 UA

NORWAY 1,162,500 UA

DENMARK 635,500 UA

IRELAND 77,500 UA

PAYMENT OF THESE CONTRIBUTIONS SHALL TAKE PLACE IN THREE EQUAL ANNUAL INSTALMENTS BEGINNING ON ACCESSION .

EACH INSTALMENT SHALL BE PAID IN THE FREELY CONVERTIBLE NATIONAL CURRENCY OF EACH NEW MEMBER STATE .
