Official Journal L 073 , 27/03/1972 P. 0033

FOR SUCH TIME AS THE UNITED KINGDOM , PURSUANT TO ARTICLE 54 , RETAINS PRODUCTION SUBSIDIES FOR SLAUGHTER CATTLE , IRELAND IS AUTHORIZED , IN ORDER TO AVOID DISTORTION OF THE IRISH CATTLE MARKET , TO RETAIN THE MEASURES RELATING TO THE EXPORT OF BEEF AND VEAL WHICH IT APPLIED BEFORE ACCESSION , IN CORRELATION WITH THE SYSTEM OF SUBSIDIES APPLIED IN THE UNITED KINGDOM .
