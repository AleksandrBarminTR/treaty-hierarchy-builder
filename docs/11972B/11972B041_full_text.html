DOCUMENTS CONCERNING THE ACCESSION TO THE EUROPEAN COMMUNITIES OF THE KINGDOM OF DENMARK, IRELAND, THE KINGDOM OF NORWAY AND THE UNITED KINGDOM OF GREAT BRITAIN AND NORTHERN IRELAND, ACT CONCERNING THE CONDITIONS OF ACCESSION AND THE ADJUSTMENTS TO THE TREATIES, PART FOUR TRANSITIONAL MEASURES, TITLE I FREE MOVEMENT OF GOODS, CHAPTER 1 TARIFF PROVISIONS, ARTICLE 41

Official Journal L 073 , 27/03/1972 P. 0023


++++

IN ORDER TO BRING THEIR TARIFFS INTO LINE WITH THE COMMON CUSTOMS TARIFF AND THE ECSC UNIFIED TARIFF , THE NEW MEMBER STATES SHALL REMAIN FREE TO ALTER THEIR CUSTOMS DUTIES MORE RAPIDLY THAN IS PROVIDED FOR IN ARTICLE 39 ( 1 ) AND ( 3 ) . THEY SHALL INFORM THE OTHER MEMBER STATES AND THE COMMISSION THEREOF .
