DOCUMENTS CONCERNING THE ACCESSION TO THE EUROPEAN COMMUNITIES OF THE KINGDOM OF DENMARK, IRELAND, THE KINGDOM OF NORWAY AND THE UNITED KINGDOM OF GREAT BRITAIN AND NORTHERN IRELAND, FINAL ACT, JOINT DECLARATION OF THE COURT OF JUSTICE

Official Journal L 073 , 27/03/1972 P. 0194


++++

SUCH ADDITIONAL MEASURES AS MAY PROVE NECESSARY FOLLOWING THE ACCESSION OF THE NEW MEMBER STATES SHOULD BE TAKEN BY THE COUNCIL WHICH , AT THE REQUEST OF THE COURT , MAY INCREASE THE NUMBER OF ADVOCATES-GENERAL TO FOUR AND ADJUST THE PROVISIONS OF THE THIRD PARAGRAPH OF ARTICLE 32 OF THE ECSC TREATY , THE THIRD PARAGRAPH OF ARTICLE 165 OF THE EEC TREATY AND THE THIRD PARAGRAPH OF ARTICLE 137 OF THE EURATOM TREATY ACCORDINGLY .
