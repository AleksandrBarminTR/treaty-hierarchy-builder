11972B/PRO/12

DOCUMENTS CONCERNING THE ACCESSION TO THE EUROPEAN COMMUNITIES OF THE KINGDOM OF DENMARK, IRELAND, THE KINGDOM OF NORWAY AND THE UNITED KINGDOM OF GREAT BRITAIN AND NORTHERN IRELAND, PROTOCOL NO 12 ON WOOD PULP ( CCT SUBHEADING NO 47.01 A II )

Official Journal L 073 , 27/03/1972 P. 0170


++++

1 . THE AUTONOMOUS DUTY IN THE COMMON CUSTOMS TARIFF ON WOOD PULP ( CCT SUBHEADING NO 47.01 A II ) SHALL BE TOTALLY SUSPENDED ACCORDING TO A TIMETABLE TO BE DETERMINED .

2 . UNTIL THE DATE OF TOTAL SUSPENSION OF THE ABOVE-MENTIONED DUTY , THE MEMBER STATES ARE AUTHORIZED TO OPEN NIL DUTY TARIFF QUOTAS IN RESPECT OF PRODUCTS COVERED BY PARAGRAPH 1 . THEY SHALL INFORM THE COMMISSION THEREOF .
