THE PROVISIONS RELATING TO THE COMMON QUALITY STANDARDS SHALL APPLY TO THE MARKETING OF HOME PRODUCE IN THE UNITED KINGDOM ONLY FROM :

( A ) 1 FEBRUARY 1974 , IN RESPECT OF ARTICHOKES , ASPARAGUS , BRUSSELS SPROUTS , RIBBED CELERY , WITLOOF CHICORY , GARLIC AND ONIONS ;

( B ) 1 FEBRUARY 1975 , IN RESPECT OF BEANS , ROUND-HEADED CABBAGES , CARROTS , LETTUCES , CURLED-LEAVED ENDIVES AND BROAD-LEAVED ( BATAVIAN ) ENDIVES , SHELLING PEAS , SPINACH AND STRAWBERRIES .
