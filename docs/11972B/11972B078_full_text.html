DOCUMENTS CONCERNING THE ACCESSION TO THE EUROPEAN COMMUNITIES OF THE KINGDOM OF DENMARK, IRELAND, THE KINGDOM OF NORWAY AND THE UNITED KINGDOM OF GREAT BRITAIN AND NORTHERN IRELAND, ACT CONCERNING THE CONDITIONS OF ACCESSION AND THE ADJUSTMENTS TO THE TREATIES, PART FOUR TRANSITIONAL MEASURES, TITLE II AGRICULTURE, CHAPTER 2 PROVISIONS RELATING TO CERTAIN COMMON ORGANIZATIONS OF MARKETS, SECTION 6 EGGS, ARTICLE 78

Official Journal L 073 , 27/03/1972 P. 0031


++++

WITH REGARD TO EGG-MARKETING STANDARDS , IRELAND AND THE UNITED KINGDOM MAY RETAIN ON THEIR MARKETS A SYSTEM OF GRADING IN FOUR AND FIVE WEIGHT-CATEGORIES RESPECTIVELY , ON CONDITION THAT THE MARKETING OF EGGS WHICH COMPLY WITH COMMUNITY STANDARDS SHALL NOT BE SUBJECT TO RESTRICTIONS BECAUSE OF DIFFERENT SYSTEMS OF GRADING .
